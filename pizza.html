ALL TIME FAVOURITES

CHICAGO DEEP DISH

A hearty and indulgent creation, Chicago deep-dish pizza is characterized by its thick, buttery crust that lines a deep pan. Layers of mozzarella cheese, meat, and chunky tomato sauce are assembled in a reverse order, with the sauce often generously ladled on top. The result is a pizza with a rich, satisfying combination of flavors and textures.

common toppings:

  • Sausage and Mushroom: Sweet Italian sausage combined with earthy mushrooms, layered over mozzarella and tomato sauce.
  • Spinach and Artichoke: A rich blend of creamy spinach and marinated artichoke hearts atop layers of cheese and sauce.
